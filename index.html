GitHub 		I am a PhD candidate and Teaching Associate at the University of Nottingham. My research interests lie in applied and environmental economics, price-setting, industrial development, and industrial structural change.
As part of my ongoing research, I am analysing the effects of energy prices on firm-level productivity.
I completed a one-year PhD traineeship at the European Central Bank, Directorate General Research (DG/R) and the Price-setting Microdata Analysis Network (PRISMA).
