Welcome to the academic website of

Juergen Amann.

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Nottingham. My research interests lie in the fields of applied econometrics, micro-level price setting as well as industrial development and industrial structural change. Recently I completed a one-year PhD traineeship at the European Central Bank (DGR).


Learn more about me, my research and teaching profiles or take a look at the online resources. The updates page provides a quick and informal overview on what I've been up to recently.
