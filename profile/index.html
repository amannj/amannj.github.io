Profile


I am a PhD candidate at the University of Nottingham. My research interests lie in the fields of applied econometrics, micro-level price setting as well as industrial development and industrial structural change. Recently I completed a one-year PhD traineeship at the European Central Bank (DGR).

I have previously studied at Duke University, the University of Manchester as well as the Vienna University of Economics and Business (WU) and hold master's degrees in economics and econometrics.

My professional experience includes a graduate teaching assistantship at Nottingham, an extensive and ongoing consultancy with the United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO) and a previously held position as research assistant at Duke.
